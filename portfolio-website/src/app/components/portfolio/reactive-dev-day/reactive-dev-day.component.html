About

During my time interning at Farm Credit Canada, I had the opportunity to create a Development Day. Dev Days were a monthly day-long development event at FCC with the purpose of educating and exposing developments to new or useful technologies. I spent a few months researching and putting together exercises on Angular's Reactive Forms API, which we were just starting to use at FCC. I tried to make the exercises fun but also informative, which is no easy task for forms.

Angular's Reactive Forms API is one of the solutions that Angular provides for constructing form models. I developed 9 exercises covering the following topics:

  • The Basics
  • Form Builder
  • Nested Forms
  • Form Arrays
  • Validation
  • Form States
  • Custom and Async Validation
  • Dynamic Forms
  • Custom Controls
