About

For my Computer Science 281 Website Design and Development class, I worked with a group of three other students throughout the semester to develop this website from the ground up. It is a portfolio website for a Mathematics and Statistics professor at the University of Saskatchewan that showcases her background, research, publications, classes, and contact info.

We met with the professor several times during the design process to ensure we properly developed what she wanted, and to pitch our design ideas and mock-ups.

The site features a navigation bar that fixes to the top of the screen if the page is scrolled down, nested collapsible information panels, easy insertion of new data or text, and a data table of publications that is sortable based on name or date.

Technologies
