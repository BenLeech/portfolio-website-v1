University Professor Professional Portfolio

Website Design and Development

For my Computer Science 281 Website Design and Development class, I worked with a group of three other students throughout the semester to develop this website. It is a portfolio website for a Mathematics and Statistics professor at the University of Saskatchewan that showcases her background, research, publications, classes, and contact info.

We met with the professor several times during the design process to ensure we properly developed what she wanted, and to pitch our design ideas and mock-ups.
